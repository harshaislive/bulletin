## Slide Copy

BI - Workflows BI 04 The 4 Workflows Prospect to pipeline. 1. AI lead qualification & routing. 2. Calendly-Zoom-Pipedrive sync. 3. Call data capture. 4. WhatsApp reschedule handling. 01 1-on-1 Flow — AI analyzes lead reason, sends fit message with pre-booked call or webinar redirect. 02 Calendly → Zoom — Auto-registers, creates activity, updates deal with unique meeting URL. 03 Call Capture — Fetches scheduled calls, stores in data table, creates Pipedrive activity. 04 Reschedule — WhatsApp trigger auto-cancels pre-booked meetings, keeps calendars clean.
