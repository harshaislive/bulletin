## Usage Guidelines

### Primary Colors
- Use Primary Green for main brand elements, headers, and primary CTAs.
- Light Green works well for hover states and secondary elements.
- Forest Green for dark backgrounds and contrast elements.

### Earth Tones
- Warm Brown for natural accents and decorative elements.
- Light Sand for background variations.
- Deep Earth for text on light backgrounds.

### Accent Colors
- Sage for subtle highlights and borders.
- Cream for background variations and cards.
- Terracotta for highlighting important elements and CTAs.

### Neutral Colors
- Off White as the primary background color.
- Light Gray for borders and dividers.
- Dark Gray for body text and secondary content.

## Presentation Mapping

- Backgrounds use Off White and Light Sand.
- Headings and primary interface elements use Primary Green and Forest Green.
- Cards and soft surfaces use Cream.
- Borders and subtle dividers use Light Gray and Sage.
- Accent states and progress highlights use Light Green and Terracotta.
- Body copy uses Dark Gray and Deep Earth for readability.
