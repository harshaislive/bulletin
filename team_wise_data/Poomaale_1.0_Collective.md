# Poomaale 1.0 Collective

## Activity

- **Pepper Harvesting:** Harvested a total of 124 kg of green pepper.
- **Robusta Coffee Picking:** Harvested a total of 15,607 kg of Robusta coffee.
- **Wheel Base Soil Filling:** Carried out wheel base soil filling work across the entire Poomaale 1.0 area.
- **Balya Kere Bund Stabilisation:** Completed bund stabilisation work, increasing the bund size. Overflow channel construction planned as the next step.
- **Waste Management:** Waste management activities undertaken (ongoing).

## Output

- 124 kg green pepper harvested.
- 15,607 kg Robusta coffee harvested.
- Wheel base soil filling completed across Poomaale 1.0.
- Balya Kere bund size increased; overflow channel planned.

## Outcome

- Significant Robusta coffee harvest volume achieved, contributing directly to produce output for the month.
- Soil filling and bund stabilisation improve road usability and water retention infrastructure on the estate.
- Bund overflow channel planning ensures long-term water management and flood risk mitigation.

## Impact

- A harvest of 15,607 kg of Robusta coffee represents a major contribution to Bewild Produce's supply pipeline.
- Infrastructure works (bund stabilisation, soil filling) enhance the long-term health and accessibility of the Poomaale 1.0 landscape.
