# Hospitality

## Headline
Hospitality became more thoughtful at the level guests actually feel.

## Subhead
Food, service, and arrival experience all improved in ways that sharpen Blyton's identity.

## Activity
Introduced new menu items, launched kombucha, tested table service, improved the approach road, and hosted a multiday guest group.

## Output
1 beverage launched, 2 more flavours stayed in review, new menu additions were served, road access improved, and monthly revenue reached Rs. 2.65 lakhs.

## Outcome
Guests encountered a more personal, more polished, and more nature-linked hospitality experience.

## Impact
Blyton is growing into a stronger hospitality brand with clearer character and healthier commercial momentum.
