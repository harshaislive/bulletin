# Human Resources

## Headline
Capability grew with more intention, not just more hiring.

## Subhead
HR used the month to bring in new people, align them early, and reinforce operating culture.

## Activity
Onboarded 4 new team members, ran Coffee with CEO, launched Fitness Sankalp, and tightened internal communication around attendance and hiring.

## Output
4 new hires joined, 1 leadership session was held, and 1 well-being initiative was launched across the organisation.

## Outcome
The team gained new capability while new joiners entered with better cultural alignment and clearer expectations.

## Impact
HR is helping Beforest scale without losing coherence, accountability, or care.
