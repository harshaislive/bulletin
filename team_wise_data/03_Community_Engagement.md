# Team: Community Experience (CE)

## Activity
- Feb 22 — First-Ever Forest Ranger Experience, Bhopal: Hosted 12 participants for a full-day trial run including vegetable harvesting, nature walk, bush lunch, biodiversity board creation, tiger and cheetal pugmark tracing with plaster of paris, and a sunset chai & samosa session on the grassland. Camping was also tested during this event.
- Feb 28–Mar 1 — Starry Nights Experience, Mumbai: Fully subscribed event with 21 participants. Activities included discovery of a new waterfall trail, a water activity explaining the concept of check dams, and telescope sessions covering planets, constellations, nebulae, and deep sky objects.
- Hands & Soil (7 Feb & 28 Feb): Conducted 2 farm volunteering sessions at Sat farm with support from the Hyderabad Collective team. Led by Sai Ram, participants joined hands-on agricultural work.
- Mumbai Collective — Registration & Buyback: Processed buyback for 12 members and completed new plot registrations for 5 members.

## Output
- 3 experience events executed (Bhopal trial, Starry Nights Mumbai, 2x Hands & Soil).
- 33 total participants engaged across experiences (12 + 21).
- 2 Hands & Soil volunteering sessions completed.
- Buyback completed for 12 members; 5 new plot registrations processed.
- 1 new waterfall trail discovered by the collective team.
- Camping format successfully tested for the first time.

## Outcome
- The Bhopal pilot delivered strong participant feedback on landscape diversity and food, providing key learnings to refine the experience format going forward.
- The Starry Nights experience was fully subscribed and participants specifically highlighted Beforest's scientific conservation approach as a standout.
- Hands & Soil sessions deepened participant connection to the land and farm practices.
- Mumbai Collective membership activity reflects active portfolio movement and continued member trust in the platform.

## Impact
- Successful pilot events in Bhopal and Mumbai establish replicable, scalable experience formats across collectives.
- Participant appreciation of Beforest's scientific approach strengthens brand credibility and conservation narrative.
- Consistent execution of community experiences reinforces the 4 Return philosophy — turning land into living, participatory ecosystems.
- Membership buyback and new registrations demonstrate financial activity and confidence in the collective model.
