# Team: Poomaale 2.0 Collective

## Activity
- Cardamom Input Application: Applied bio-inputs to cardamom plants across the collective.
- Shade Looping Clearing: Cleared coffee areas following completion of shade looping work.
- Robusta De-suckering: Carried out de-suckering and topping of Robusta coffee plants.
- Road Work – Curing: Road curing work carried out on the estate.
- Safety Cage for Transformer: Installed a safety cage enclosure around the transformer for protection.

## Output
- Bio-input application completed on cardamom plants.
- Coffee areas cleared post shade looping.
- Robusta coffee plants de-suckered and topped.
- Road curing work completed.
- Safety cage installed around the transformer.

## Outcome
- Cardamom plants are nourished and prepared for healthy growth through the season.
- Cleared coffee areas post shade looping allow better light penetration and airflow, improving crop conditions.
- De-suckering and topping of Robusta plants directs energy to productive stems and improves yield potential.
- Road curing ensures durability and usability of estate roads.
- Transformer safety cage reduces risk of electrical accidents for workers and wildlife on the estate.

## Impact
- Systematic agronomic care (bio-inputs, de-suckering, shade clearing) across Poomaale 2.0 sets a strong foundation for crop productivity in the coming months.
- Safety and infrastructure improvements (transformer cage, road curing) reflect a maturing operational standard at the collective.
