# Bhopal Collective

## Headline
Ground work and guest experience moved forward together.

## Subhead
Bhopal improved access on the land while testing a new experience format for visitors.

## Activity
Built trail bridges, managed crops across 10 acres, improved soil health, and ran the first Forest Ranger experience.

## Output
5 bridges installed, 3 crops managed, soil inputs applied, and 12 participants hosted in the pilot event.

## Outcome
The land became safer to move through, farm operations stayed on track, and the team learned what a repeatable experience format could look like.

## Impact
Bhopal is becoming both more self-sustaining on the ground and more engaging as a place people can experience.
