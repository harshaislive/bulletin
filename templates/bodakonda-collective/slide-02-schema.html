Bodakonda Collective
02

What's the story Cultivation, systems, and shared labor all advancing together.

Activity

Handled paddy work, vegetable harvesting, drip setup, apiculture flora planting, fencing, composting, bio-inputs, terrace care, and volunteer sessions.

Output

880 kg of vegetables were harvested, 800 metres of trenching were completed, multiple terrace interventions were executed, and 2 volunteer sessions were run.

Outcome

The collective became more productive, more water-efficient, more biologically supportive, and more participatory.

Impact

Bodakonda is shaping up as a strong example of ecology-first cultivation with community involvement built in.
