What's the story Major harvest output with underlying estate improvements.

Activity

Harvested pepper and robusta, carried out soil filling, stabilised the bund, and continued waste management work.

Output

124 kg of green pepper and 15,607 kg of robusta were harvested, while soil filling and bund work progressed across the estate.

Outcome

Produce output remained strong and key estate infrastructure became more stable and usable.

Impact

Poomaale 1.0 continues to be a major supply landscape while strengthening the base conditions that support it.
