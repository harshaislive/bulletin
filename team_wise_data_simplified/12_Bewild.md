# Bewild

## Headline
Bewild became easier to buy, easier to notice, and easier to trust.

## Subhead
The month moved on three fronts at once: a clearer storefront, stronger product momentum, and more visible presence in the market.

## Activity
Reworked the website, launched new products, restarted veggie bags, improved on-ground systems, tested new coffee fermentation, and took the brand into more physical spaces.

## Output
3 new products went live, veggie bags restarted strongly, 2 co-fermentation batches progressed, 3 city activations happened, and cafe presence expanded.

## Outcome
Customers could discover the brand more easily, buy with less friction, and encounter Bewild both online and in real life.

## Impact
Bewild is no longer only a product catalog. It is beginning to behave like a real market-facing brand.
