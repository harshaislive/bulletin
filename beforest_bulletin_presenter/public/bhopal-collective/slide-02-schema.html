Bhopal Collective
02

What happened

Infrastructure, agriculture, and experience pilots.

5 Bridges installed
10 Acres cultivated
12 Experience guests
Activity

Installed five wooden bridges along the nature trail. Carried out agricultural operations across 10 acres: harvesting Pigeon Pea (4 acres), irrigating Mustard (2 acres), and two Wheat varieties — Sharbati and Khapail. Applied manure and Jeevamrit. Hosted first Forest Ranger Experience Event with 12 participants.

Output

5 wooden bridges functional on nature trail. 3 active crops under management. 1 pilot experience event completed. Soil enrichment applied across agricultural plots.

Outcome

Nature trail is now safe and accessible year-round. Agricultural productivity supported through timely inputs. Team gained direct learnings from first experience pilot.

Impact

Improved visitor experience with safe trail crossings. 10 acres contributes to operational self-sufficiency. First pilot establishes a replicable experience program opening new engagement and revenue channel.
