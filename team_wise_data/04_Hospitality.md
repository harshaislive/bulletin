# Team: Hospitality

## Activity
- Trialled and introduced new food and beverage items for guests at Blyton, including menu additions and refreshment upgrades.
- Officially launched Kombucha (Ginger & Mint flavour) as a refreshment drink at Blyton after 2 development iterations. Additional flavours (Coffee, Green Pepper) are currently under review.
- Implemented table service during low-occupancy periods as a strategy to reduce food waste.
- Harvested broad beans from the garden and served them fresh in meals; added beetroot raita to the menu.
- Carried out road improvement works — engaged a JCB to level the uneven approach road entry (previously rocky and sharp-edged), improving guest access. Upcoming plan to pave the area while retaining its rustic character.
- Hosted a senior citizen group (IIT Kanpur alumni) for a 4-day stay — a lively and high-energy group.

## Output
- 1 new beverage (Kombucha – Ginger & Mint) officially launched.
- 2 additional Kombucha flavours in review pipeline (Coffee, Green Pepper).
- 2 new menu additions: fresh broad beans dish and beetroot raita.
- Table service model trialled and implemented during low-guest periods.
- Approach road levelled and access improved; paving plan in progress.
- 1 multi-day group stay successfully hosted (4 days, senior citizen / IIT Kanpur alumni group).
- Revenue for the month: ₹2.65 Lakhs.

## Outcome
- Guests experienced an elevated food and beverage offering with garden-fresh produce and house-made drinks.
- Table service during quieter periods reduced food waste and improved the quality of guest interaction.
- Improved road access creates a smoother and more welcoming arrival experience for all guests.
- The senior citizen group stay added vibrant energy to Blyton and demonstrated the property's ability to host diverse guest profiles.

## Impact
- In-house Kombucha production and farm-to-table additions strengthen Blyton's identity as a conscious, nature-integrated hospitality destination.
- Waste reduction through adaptive service models reflects operational maturity and alignment with sustainability values.
- Infrastructure improvements at the approach road directly enhance first impressions and long-term guest satisfaction.
- Strong monthly revenue of ₹2.65L, alongside a new group guest segment, signals healthy commercial momentum for the hospitality vertical.
