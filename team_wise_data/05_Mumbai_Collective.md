# Team: Mumbai Collective

## Activity
- Mulching: Completed mulching for 443 plants in the trial plot plantation to conserve soil moisture, regulate soil temperature, improve soil health, and reduce weed growth.
- Watering: Watered a total of 2,571 plants across CZ-1 (197 plants), Banana (40 plants), EZ-1 (226 plants), and the trial plot (1,033 plants) — using approximately 51,420 litres of water.
- Fence Repair: Repaired 676 RFT of fencing across Banana plantation (145 RFT), trial plot (110 RFT), and EZ-1 plantation (421 RFT) to protect crops from cows and buffaloes.
- NPK Solution Application: Applied NPK fertiliser solution to 1,345 plants across CZ-1 (95), EZ-1 (203), and trial plot (1,047) to promote healthy growth.
- Solar Fencing Installation: Installed solar fencing covering 1,741 RFT around the trial plot plantation to protect plants from cattle.
- Vegetable Beds: Prepared vegetable beds over approximately 250 RFT in the staff patch to support vegetable cultivation.
- Nursery Maintenance: Completed watering and external cleaning of the nursery net to maintain moisture levels and a clean growing environment.
- Nature Trail Preparation: Built natural steps and completed weeding along 782 RFT of the nature trail in preparation for the Starry Nights event.
- Starry Nights Experience (Feb 28–Mar 1): Hosted participants over the weekend with activities including telescope sessions (planets, constellations, galaxies, deep-sky objects), a hike to a hidden waterfall spot, water conservation exploration, and mindfulness journaling.

## Output
- 443 plants mulched.
- 2,571 plants watered using ~51,420 litres of water.
- 676 RFT of fencing repaired across 3 zones.
- 1,345 plants treated with NPK solution.
- 1,741 RFT of solar fencing installed around the trial plot.
- 250 RFT of vegetable beds prepared in the staff patch.
- Nursery watering and cleaning completed.
- 782 RFT of nature trail prepared (steps + weeding) for Starry Nights.
- Starry Nights experience successfully hosted with 21 participants.

## Outcome
- Comprehensive soil and plant care across all plantation zones (CZ-1, EZ-1, Banana, trial plot) has improved moisture retention, nutrient availability, and crop protection.
- Solar and physical fencing upgrades significantly reduce risk of livestock damage to crops.
- Vegetable bed preparation enables fresh produce cultivation at the collective.
- Nature trail improvements enhanced the participant experience at the Starry Nights event.
- Participants gained hands-on understanding of conservation, astronomy, and mindfulness — strengthening their connection to the landscape.

## Impact
- Systematic land and plant management across 2,500+ plants reflects the Mumbai Collective's growing operational scale and ecological commitment.
- Infrastructure investments (solar fencing, vegetable beds, trail improvements) build long-term resilience and self-sufficiency of the collective.
- The fully subscribed Starry Nights experience reinforces the Mumbai Collective as a premium destination, with participant feedback highlighting Beforest's scientific conservation approach as a standout.
