# Community Experience

## Headline
Experiences are becoming a repeatable part of the Beforest model.

## Subhead
This month turned participation into something more structured across Bhopal and Mumbai.

## Activity
Ran the Forest Ranger pilot, hosted Starry Nights, conducted Hands & Soil sessions, and processed buybacks and new registrations.

## Output
Multiple experience formats were delivered, 33 participants engaged, 2 volunteering sessions completed, and membership movement was processed.

## Outcome
People connected more deeply to the land, while the team gained clearer evidence of what works in live experiences.

## Impact
Community participation is becoming a real layer of the Beforest offering, not an occasional add-on.
