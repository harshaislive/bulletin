What happened

Crop, boundary, infrastructure.

44 Acres weeded
2.6K Sq ft pond
1.2 Km road started
Activity

Machine weeding across 6 Arabica blocks (44 acres). Complete Sy. No. survey, GPS marking, boundary fencing and poles. Sprinkler irrigation for cardamom. Bio-input NPK in Block 4. BT spray for shoot borer. 8 HP motor repaired, movable trolley installed, water tank covered. JCB road work commenced (1.2 km). Pond expanded to 2,600 sq ft.

Output

44 acres weeded. Complete survey, GPS, boundary done. Sprinkler irrigation operational. Bio-input and pest control applied. Motor repaired. 1.2 km road work started. Pond expanded to 2,00,000 litre capacity.

Outcome

Arabica blocks clean and weed-free. Land survey provides legal clarity, protects from encroachment. Cardamom well-irrigated, nourished, pest-managed. Water infrastructure more reliable. Road will improve connectivity. Pond enhances water security.

Impact

Comprehensive management strengthens productivity and legal ownership. Pre-monsoon prep positions cardamom for stronger yield. Infrastructure (roads, water, pond) enhances operational and ecological resilience.
