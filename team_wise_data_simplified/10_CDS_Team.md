# CD&S

## Headline
Science became easier to see and easier to use.

## Subhead
CD&S translated complex work into tools, stories, and protocols that others can act on.

## Activity
Published a Hammiyala GIS story, advanced the Bhopal version, trained farm teams on microbial inputs, built crop reference material, and planned biodiversity surveys.

## Output
1 GIS story went live, another stayed in development, 800 litres of microbial inputs were applied, and a 4-collective survey calendar was created.

## Outcome
Teams now have clearer ways to understand landscapes, manage field practice, and prepare for biodiversity data collection.

## Impact
CD&S strengthens Beforest's science-backed credibility by making knowledge more visible, practical, and shareable.
