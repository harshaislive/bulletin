# Team: BI (Business Intelligence)

## Activity
- **1-on-1 Flow Synthesize & Message:** Built a workflow that processes responses from leads who opt for a 1-on-1 interaction. An AI agent analyses their reason for joining the collective and automatically sends a "Fit" message with a pre-booked call link or redirects them to a webinar registration if they are not a fit. The previous 1-on-1 activity is then auto-marked as completed.
- **Calendly to Zoom – Add Registrant to Meeting:** Developed a workflow that automatically registers participants in Zoom upon booking through Calendly. It simultaneously creates a Pipedrive activity titled "Added to [Collective Name] Webinar – [Date & Time]" and updates the corresponding deal with the participant's unique Zoom meeting URL for accurate tracking and follow-up.
- **Polling Rakesh & Vivek's Calendar for 1-on-1 Captures to Data Tables:** Built a workflow that fetches all scheduled 1-on-1 calls booked by deals, stores the data in a data table, and creates a corresponding Pipedrive activity for leads who have scheduled a 1-on-1 call with the owner.
- **Interakt API Messages Status Dump:** Created a workflow that monitors WhatsApp responses where leads choose the "Reschedule a Call" option from the 1-on-1 synthesis automation. When triggered, it automatically cancels the pre-booked 1-on-1 meeting from the owner's calendar, preventing scheduling conflicts.

## Output
- 4 end-to-end automation workflows built and deployed.
- AI-powered lead qualification and routing system live for 1-on-1 interactions.
- Calendly-Zoom-Pipedrive integration fully automated for webinar registrations.
- 1-on-1 call data automatically captured and stored in data tables with Pipedrive activity creation.
- WhatsApp-triggered rescheduling automation live, synced with owner calendars.

## Outcome
- Lead qualification and routing is now fully automated — reducing manual effort and ensuring faster, more accurate follow-up for every prospect interaction.
- Webinar registrations are seamlessly handled end-to-end with no manual intervention required, and every deal in Pipedrive is updated with accurate tracking links.
- 1-on-1 call data is systematically captured and organised, giving the sales team a clear view of scheduled interactions.
- Calendar conflicts from rescheduling requests are automatically resolved, keeping owner schedules clean and accurate.

## Impact
- The 4 automation workflows collectively eliminate significant manual workload across lead management, webinar coordination, and calendar management.
- AI-driven lead qualification ensures prospects are routed to the right next step instantly — improving conversion potential and prospect experience.
- End-to-end CRM and calendar hygiene through automation gives leadership real-time, reliable visibility into the sales pipeline.
