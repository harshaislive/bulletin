# BI / Business Intelligence

## Headline
Quiet systems work removed friction from growth and helped Bewild become more legible.

## Subhead
BI improved how prospects move through calls, webinars, calendars, and follow-up, while also supporting Bewild's digital and reporting backbone.

## Activity
Built automations for lead qualification, webinar registration, call capture, and reschedule handling across AI, Calendly, Zoom, Pipedrive, and WhatsApp. BI also supported Bewild through website, sales reporting, and automated weekly reporting systems.

## Output
4 end-to-end workflows were deployed, key prospect journeys became automated, and Bewild's reporting and digital visibility systems were strengthened.

## Outcome
Follow-up became faster, cleaner, and more reliable with less manual intervention, while Bewild gained better reporting rhythm and stronger digital support.

## Impact
BI is making scale feel more orderly by turning complex coordination into quiet, dependable systems and by giving Bewild a more reliable commercial operating layer.
