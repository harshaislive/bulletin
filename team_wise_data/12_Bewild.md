# Team: Bewild

## Activity
- **Website Revamp – Home & Product Pages:** Revamped the Home Page and Product Pages for both mobile and desktop in collaboration with the BI Team to improve navigation, simplify product discovery, and create a smoother buying experience for customers.
- **New Products Launched on Website:** Listed 3 new products on the Bewild website — Amla Pickle (Naturally Fermented, 250g – Rs. 320), Peanut Butter (Crunchy, 250g – Rs. 349), and Bajra Laddu (Hand Rolled, 250g – Rs. 379) — expanding the range of native, forest-friendly everyday pantry products.
- **Veggie Bags Restarted from Hyderabad Collective:** After a 7-month pause, Veggie Bags from the Hyderabad Collective were relaunched. In the first week, 19 out of 20 bags were sold out, with the remaining produce sold separately.
- **Rosella + Robusta Co-Fermentation Trial:** Two experimental co-fermentation batches blending Poomaale (Robusta) and Bodakonda (Rosella) have completed drying and are currently in the resting phase, moving toward cupping and sensory evaluation.
- **Poomaale – Strengthening Systems On-Ground:** Initiated new human-centric SOPs with executive training on equipment usage. Checklists updated to include local languages for improved clarity and operational efficiency. Introduced a structured issue-tracker system to streamline B2B2C order processing at Blyton and strengthen cross-functional coordination.
- **Sensory Evaluation Session – Grab & Go Offering:** Conducted a sensory evaluation session at HO with samples from a potential partner for the upcoming Grab & Go space at the Experience Center, assessing taste, quality, and brand alignment.
- **Bewild Featured in "Our Bangalore":** Bewild Coffee and the work at Poomaale Collective were featured in a print editorial by Bindu Gopal Rao in Our Bangalore (Feb 14–20, 2026 edition), highlighting the journey from landscape to cup with a focus on forest-friendly growing practices.
- **Bewild Stall at Total Environment, Hyderabad (8 Feb 2026):** Set up a stall at Total Environment, Hyderabad, continuing to connect with like-minded consumers who resonate with forest-friendly, traceable produce.
- **Team Bewild at IICF 2026, Bengaluru (12–13 Feb):** Attended India's largest coffee festival to engage with industry stakeholders, explore collaborations, and stay updated on emerging trends in sustainable and specialty food systems.
- **Bewild's First Stall at Piramal Towers, Mumbai (25 Feb 2026):** Set up the first-ever Bewild stall at Piramal Towers, Mumbai, led on-ground by the Mumbai Collective team, with plans to continue for the next 4–6 weeks.
- **Bewild Robusta Naturals Now at Naad Coffee, Hyderabad:** Following True Black, Bewild Robusta Naturals is now available at Naad Coffee, Hyderabad, expanding café presence and local access to landscape-grown coffee from Poomaale.

## Output
- Bewild website Home and Product Pages fully revamped for mobile and desktop.
- 3 new products live on the website (Amla Pickle, Peanut Butter, Bajra Laddu).
- Veggie Bags relaunched — 19/20 bags sold out in the first week.
- 2 Rosella + Robusta co-fermentation batches completed drying; in resting phase.
- New SOPs initiated; checklists updated in local languages; issue-tracker system introduced at Poomaale.
- 1 sensory evaluation session conducted for Grab & Go partner shortlisting.
- 1 print media feature published in Our Bangalore (Feb 14–20, 2026).
- 3 physical stall activations completed (Total Environment Hyderabad, IICF Bengaluru, Piramal Towers Mumbai).
- Bewild Robusta Naturals listed at Naad Coffee, Hyderabad.

## Outcome
- The website revamp makes Bewild's digital storefront more intuitive, improving product discovery and the overall buying experience for customers.
- New product additions diversify the Bewild range with native, traditionally rooted pantry options.
- The strong Veggie Bag relaunch response signals renewed customer trust and demand for fresh, chemical-free, locally sourced produce.
- The co-fermentation trial pushes process-led innovation forward, creating a first-of-its-kind product that blends two Beforest landscapes into a single cup.
- On-ground system improvements at Poomaale are building more efficient, scalable, and inclusive operations.
- The sensory evaluation session moves the Grab & Go offering closer to launch with a values-aligned partner shortlist.
- Print media visibility in Our Bangalore brings Bewild's conservation and coffee story to a wider audience.
- Physical stall activations in Hyderabad, Bengaluru, and Mumbai expand Bewild's presence across key markets and connect directly with conscious consumers.
- The Naad Coffee listing deepens Bewild's café channel presence in Hyderabad.

## Impact
- Website improvements and new product listings directly support better conversion and customer retention on Bewild's digital platform.
- The Veggie Bag relaunch reopens a high-trust, recurring revenue channel connecting collective produce directly to consumers.
- The co-fermentation trial positions Bewild as an innovator in India's specialty coffee space, differentiating the brand through terroir-led, process-driven storytelling.
- Multi-city stall activations and café listings expand Bewild's physical footprint and brand visibility, supporting customer acquisition across Hyderabad, Mumbai, and Bengaluru.
- The Our Bangalore feature and IICF participation strengthen Bewild's credibility and positioning within the sustainable and specialty food ecosystem.
