The flow: Wings → Collectives → Research, Design, Growth → Systems & Support

Wings of Beforest (Hospitality, Community Experience, Bewild) set the tone — the soul of how we welcome and create. Collectives (Bhopal, Mumbai, Hammiyala, Hyderabad, Poomaale 1.0, Poomaale 2.0) are the heart — six communities growing in their own way. Research, Design, Growth (CD&S) is the ecological design team for the entire ecosystem. Systems & Support (HR + BI) run the engine quietly in the background.

Each team deck is self-contained — present straight through or jump to specific teams. ~15-30 seconds per slide.
