# Hammiyala Collective

## Headline
Land clarity and landscape resilience advanced together.

## Subhead
Hammiyala focused on legal protection, crop care, water systems, and internal access in the same month.

## Activity
Completed weeding, survey and boundary work, irrigation and nutrient application, pest response, water system repairs, road work, and pond expansion.

## Output
44 acres were machine-weeded, survey and fencing progressed across the estate, irrigation stayed active, road work began, and the pond was expanded.

## Outcome
The estate is cleaner, more secure, better irrigated, and better connected going into the next phase.

## Impact
Hammiyala is becoming a more resilient landscape in both operational and ecological terms.
