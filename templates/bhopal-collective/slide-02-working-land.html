Bhopal / working land
02

The practical month

The first shift was simple: make the land easier to move through and easier to sustain.

Activity

Trail bridges, crop work across 10 acres, and soil care that keeps the ground productive.

Output

5 bridges installed, 3 crops managed, and soil inputs applied across the fields.

Outcome

Safer movement on site and steadier conditions for the farm to keep performing.

Impact

Bhopal feels less provisional and more ready for both work and welcome.
