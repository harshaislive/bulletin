# Mumbai Collective

## Headline
Strong field discipline made a premium experience possible.

## Subhead
The month balanced plant care, protection, and trail preparation with a fully subscribed member event.

## Activity
Mulched, watered, fenced, applied nutrients, prepared vegetable beds, maintained the nursery, improved the trail, and hosted Starry Nights.

## Output
2,571 plants watered, 1,741 RFT of solar fencing installed, 782 RFT of trail prepared, and 21 participants hosted for Starry Nights.

## Outcome
The landscape became better protected and better prepared, while participants experienced the site with more depth and coherence.

## Impact
Mumbai is emerging as both a serious working landscape and a high-quality experience destination.
